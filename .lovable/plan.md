

# TO ZERO Website Redesign

A modern, elegant redesign of your sustainable public transport website inspired by the clean, premium aesthetic of the Ather Energy site.

---

## 1. Navigation Bar
- Sticky, minimal top navigation with the TO ZERO logo on the left
- Clean nav links (Mission, Solutions, Benefits, About, Contact)
- Prominent CTA button ("Get in Touch") on the right
- Smooth transparency-to-white transition on scroll

## 2. Hero Section
- Full-width hero with a bold background image of electric buses
- Large, impactful headline: "Paving the way for sustainable public transport"
- Subtitle text with a clear value proposition
- Two CTA buttons (e.g., "Discover Our Solutions" and "Contact Us")
- Clean overlay gradient for text readability

## 3. Mission Section
- Clean white background with centered heading
- Key stats displayed in a horizontal row with large numbers and labels (e.g., fleet size, CO₂ saved, cities served)
- Brief mission statement paragraph
- Subtle animations on scroll

## 4. Solutions Section
- Section heading with brief intro text
- Interactive product/solution cards (similar to Ather's product family cards)
- Each card shows a bus image, solution name, and key specs
- Hover effects with subtle scale/shadow transitions
- "Learn More" buttons on each card

## 5. Benefits Section
- Grid of benefit cards with icons
- Clean iconography with short descriptions
- Topics like cost savings, sustainability, reliability, smart technology
- Cards with subtle hover animations

## 6. Quote / Testimonial Banner
- Full-width teal/dark section with a compelling quote
- Background image with overlay
- Creates visual contrast and breaks up the white sections

## 7. About Us Section
- Split layout: text on one side, image on the other
- Brief company description highlighting mission and values
- Clean typography with good hierarchy

## 8. Contact / CTA Section
- Eye-catching section encouraging visitors to reach out
- Simple form-like layout (name, email, message fields — static, no backend)
- Or a clear "Get in Touch" CTA linking to email/phone

## 9. FAQ Section
- Collapsible accordion-style FAQ
- Clean, minimal design matching the overall aesthetic

## 10. Footer
- Dark footer with organized link columns
- Company info, quick links, social media icons
- Copyright and legal links

---

## Design Principles
- **Color palette**: Clean whites, with teal/cyan accents (from TO ZERO branding), dark text
- **Typography**: Modern sans-serif, large headings, generous spacing
- **Whitespace**: Ample breathing room between sections
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in and slide-up effects on scroll
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly design
- **Interactive**: Hover effects on cards and buttons, smooth scrolling navigation
